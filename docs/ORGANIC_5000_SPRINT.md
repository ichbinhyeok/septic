# Organic 5K Sprint

## Goal

Reach roughly 5,000 monthly organic sessions as fast as possible without
destroying trust or widening into thin 50-state programmatic SEO.

## Working assumption

- Time horizon: 6 months
- Traffic metric: monthly organic sessions
- Best wedge: state-specific homeowner workflow + cost-intent pages
- Anti-goal: broad educational volume that is weak on source transparency

## What this sprint prioritizes

1. Strong states with a real official-source angle
   - GA
   - PA
   - CT
   - OR
   - MA
   - FL
2. High-value intent clusters
   - replacement
   - perc / site evaluation
   - buyer-side risk
   - permit path
   - records checklist
3. Internal linking that moves users from:
   - workflow page -> state guide -> calculator -> quote form

## Highest-leverage live page types

- Main calculator
- State guide
- State-specific replacement page
- State-specific perc page
- State-specific buyer page
- State-specific permit-process page
- State-specific records-checklist page

## Why these pages first

- They map to higher-intent questions than generic septic education.
- They are easier to make unique with official-source-backed state detail.
- They create natural internal links into the calculator and quote flow.
- They are less likely to look like thin state-name swaps.

## What not to do during the sprint

- Do not expand to 50 states.
- Do not create county pages at scale.
- Do not publish fake-exact gallon or code-compliance claims.
- Do not let maintenance content outrun replacement and buyer intent.

## Measurement focus

- Organic landing -> calculator clickthrough
- Calculator completion rate
- Result page -> quote form start rate
- State guide -> workflow page internal-link CTR
- Queries that show permit, records, buyer, replacement, and perc intent
