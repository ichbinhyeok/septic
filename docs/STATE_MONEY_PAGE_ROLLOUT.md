# State Money Page Rollout

Last updated: 2026-03-09

## Decision

Open state-specific money pages before broad state-by-state content expansion.

V1 launch set:

- `septic-replacement-cost`
- `perc-test-cost`

Launch states:

- Georgia
- Pennsylvania
- Connecticut
- Oregon
- Massachusetts
- Florida

This creates 12 state-specific money pages with stronger commercial intent than generic education pages.

## Why These Two Intents

- `septic-replacement-cost` sits close to a quote request and naturally supports installer matching later.
- `perc-test-cost` is lower-ticket, but it is one of the clearest ways to explain why the final project range widens.

## Why These Six States

- Georgia: strong homeowner-facing sizing and disposal rule.
- Pennsylvania: local municipality and Sewage Enforcement Officer angle.
- Connecticut: bedroom-based design flow and local health review.
- Oregon: site-evaluation-first permit sequence.
- Massachusetts: Title 5 inspection and transfer pressure.
- Florida: DEP-managed county split versus county health department routing.

## Guardrails

- Do not promise exact statewide prices.
- Keep state-specific cost pages tied to official permit and process context.
- Use commercial sources only for broad public cost anchors, not legal or code claims.
- Link each state money page back to:
  - the state guide
  - the main calculator
  - at least one related money page

## Later Expansion

Do not expand every intent across every state at once.

Next candidates after the first 12 pages:

- Massachusetts `buying-a-house-with-a-septic-system`
- Washington `septic-pumping-cost`
- New Jersey `buying-a-house-with-a-septic-system`
- Florida `drain-field-replacement-cost`
